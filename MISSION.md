# Mission

Clawrari exists to make AI assistants permanently better by open-sourcing the patterns, files, and operating loops behind a high-performance OpenClaw setup.

## What This Means

- Ship patterns that survive real daily use, not theory.
- Open the process, not just the code.
- Prefer strong defaults with clear escape hatches.
- Treat self-improvement as a product feature, not a side effect.
- Turn building in public into part of the operating system.

## Who It Serves

- Technical founders and CTOs who want an AI operating system, not a toy.
- AI engineers and power users who want a real reference implementation.
- Builders using personal AI workflows as a content and learning engine.

## Standard

If a fresh OpenClaw install can clone Clawrari, bootstrap cleanly, wire the core connectors, and feel measurably better within a day, the project is doing its job.
